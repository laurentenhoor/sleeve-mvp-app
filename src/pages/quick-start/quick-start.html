Quick Start Guide

Remove the Cap

We will weigh to measure the amount of milk. The weight of the cap disturbs the measurement.

Please retry on a flat and hard surface.

Insert the Bottle

After preparing your baby’s bottle, insert the bottle in the bottle tracker.

First Volume Measurement Before Feeding

Place the sleeve on a flat and hard surface and short press the button.

The light turns green when the feed's volume measurement is successful.

Feeding

Hold the bottle in feeding position.
After 10 seconds a feed will be detected.

The lights will be off while your tracking your baby's feeding.

Extra tip: Wiggle!

If you want to be sure that the Sleeve is tracking your feed. You can wiggle the Sleeve and the light will blink white to indicate it is tracking.

Second Volume Measurement to Finish Feeding

Press the button shortly to finish the feed. The blinking white light indicates the Smart Bottle Sleeve is ready to measure the feed’s end volume.

Syncing Data

After you've finished the feed, the data will be synced when opening the app.

The sleeve can store up to 100 feeds. The app is not needed while tracking the feed.
